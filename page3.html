Why UX?

I chose user experience due to the intersection between technology and human behavior. I myself am not necessarily a lazy person; however, I do sincerely appreciate the times when I complete an anction, whether entirely mundane or intricately complex, with ease. By studying human behavior in accordance with technology, with the intent of creating intuitive digital interactions, I hope to help open the door for a huge increase in user ease. Designing products and interfaces that enhance user satisfaction, due to increase ease of action completion, directly aligns my goal of making every day easier, while also adhereing to my creative side and my enjoyment of recognizing problems and offering solutions. My overall goal in contributing to the world of technology, with user experience as the forefront of these contributions, is to offer user-centric solutions that positively impact the interactions between humans and technology, as well as the benefits that come thereafter.
