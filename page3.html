Why UX?

I chose to pursue user experience because it represents the powerful intersection of technology and human behavior. While I would not describe myself as someone who backs away from a challenge, I deeply value moments when completing a task, whether simple or complex, feels effortless. By studying human behavior in relation to technology, and designing with the goal of creating intuitive digital experiences, I hope to drive a significant increase in user ease and satisfaction. Crafting products and interfaces that make everyday actions more seamless not only fulfills my ambition to make daily life easier, but also aligns with my creative instincts and passion for identifying problems and developing thoughtful solutions. Through my contributions to the field of user experience, I aspire to deliver user-centric innovations that meaningfully enhance how people interact with technology—and the broader benefits those improved interactions can bring.

My Goals

  • Shape the design of applications that drive meaningful, enduring societal change
  • Champion inclusive design practices to create exceptional experiences for marginalized and underrepresented communities
  • Design widely adopted applications that seamlessly integrate into users’ daily lives and deliver lasting value
  • Continuously deepen my expertise in user research methods to better understand diverse user needs and behaviors
  • Collaborate across disciplines to bridge gaps between design, engineering, and user advocacy for stronger product outcomes
  • Push the boundaries of creativity by integrating emerging technologies, such as AI or AR, into human-centered design solutions
