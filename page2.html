Direction and Transportation Details

Location: The festival takes place in Downtown Ithaca and it is specifically centered around the Itahca Commons.

Restrooms: There are six restrooms in or in close proximity of the festival. These restrooms are marked on the map with a red "R" enclosed in a blue circle.

Parking: As seen on the map there are two places that you can park in, spots permitted. The first is called "Green Street Parking" and it is located on Green St, next to the Cinemapolis Movie Theater. The second is a parking garage called the "Seneca Garage" and is located on the corner of Tioga St and Seneca St.

Transportation: You can travel to the Apple Harvest festival by many modes of transportation. For dropping off cars, ubers, and lyfts are more than welcome! By imputting Ithaca Commons into the directions, you should arrive at Apple Harvest with no difficulty. If you are college student traveling by bus, both Cornell University and Ithaca College use the TCAT bus system. Hop on Bus 90 and you should arrive in the commons with no problems! Obviously, we welcome non locomotive users, or walkers too!

We hope to see you at Ithaca's Apple Harvest Festival this year!
